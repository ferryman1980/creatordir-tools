Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jun 11, 2026

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 collects anonymized data including pages visited, time spent, browser type, and general geographic location. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of cookies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required for analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense).

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our About page.

What Data We Collect

  • Google Analytics: anonymized page views, session duration, browser type, device type, general location (city level). No names, emails, or IP addresses are stored.
  • Google AdSense: cookies for personalized advertising. See Google Ad Policies.
  • Server Logs: Vercel stores standard access logs (request URL, timestamp, status code) for up to 30 days.

How We Use Your Data

  • Improve site content based on popular pages and user behavior patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyze traffic sources to understand what content our visitors find valuable

Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Visit Google Ad Settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On
  • Cookie management: Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Vercel Inc. - Web hosting. Their privacy policy: vercel.com/privacy
  • Google LLC - Analytics and advertising. Their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Server logs are retained for 30 days. We do not maintain any user databases or collect personal information directly.
