# I Built a Free Directory of 240+ AI Tools - Here's Everything I Learned

After spending months testing and reviewing AI tools, I created **[CreatorAI Tools](https://creatordir-tools.vercel.app)** - a free, open-source directory of 240+ AI tools for content creators.

## Why I Built It

The AI tool landscape is overwhelming. Every day there's a new "revolutionary" tool claiming to transform your workflow. I wanted to cut through the noise and provide honest, hands-on reviews.

## What's Inside

- **240+ articles** covering AI tools for writing, design, video, audio, marketing, and productivity
- **54 detailed tool reviews** with pros, cons, and pricing
- **Honest comparisons** - no sponsored content, just real testing
- **Affiliate links** (Hostinger, ElevenLabs) - if you find the content useful, using these links supports the project

## Key Learnings

1. **Free tiers are surprisingly good**: ChatGPT, Canva AI, and Claude offer excellent free versions that handle most tasks
2. **Specialization matters**: The best video AI isn't the best writing AI - use the right tool for each job
3. **AI is a multiplier, not a replacement**: The best results come from human + AI collaboration
