AI Video Creation: From Script to Published in 2026

Jun 11, 2026 · 6 min read · New

I spent 3 years learning video editing the hard way. Then AI tools arrived and cut my production time by 80%. Here is my current workflow.

My 4-Step AI Video Workflow

Step 1: Script — ChatGPT

I write a rough outline, then ask ChatGPT to expand it into a full script with timing markers. A 10-minute video script takes 15 minutes instead of 2 hours.

Step 2: Voiceover — ElevenLabs

Professional voiceover in 5 minutes. Pick a voice, paste the script, download the audio. Cost: pennies per video. Quality: indistinguishable from human voiceover artists in blind tests.

Step 3: Video Editing — CapCut + Runway

CapCut for assembly: auto-captions (99% accurate), transitions, effects. Runway for AI video generation: text-to-video clips, motion brush, inpainting. Combined, they replace Adobe Premiere for 90% of content creator needs.

Step 4: Thumbnail — Canva AI or Midjourney

Canva AI generates thumbnail concepts from the video title. Pick, tweak, export. Under 5 minutes.

What Used to Take 3 Days Now Takes 4 Hours

Old workflow: write script (4h), record voiceover (2h), edit video (8h), create thumbnail (1h), publish (0.5h) = 15.5 hours. New workflow: AI script (0.25h), AI voice (0.1h), AI-assisted edit (3h), AI thumbnail (0.1h), publish (0.25h) = 3.7 hours. That is 76% faster.
