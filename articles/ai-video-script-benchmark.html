AI Video Script Writers Compared: ChatGPT vs Claude vs DeepSeek

Jun 5, 2026 · 8 min read

Writing a 60-second video script manually takes about 30 minutes. With AI, it takes 2 minutes. But which AI produces the best scripts? We tested 4 tools on the same brief: "How to make $200/month with AI side hustles in 2026" — a 60-second talking head video.

Test Results

ChatGPT — ★★★★★ Overall Best

Best opening hook: "Have you ever wondered why some people make money while they sleep?" The script followed the ideal structure: pain point → 3 methods → strong closing → call to action. Language was conversational and needed zero editing.

Time: 2 minutes

DeepSeek — ★★★★☆ Best Data

Included specific statistics: "According to the 2026 AI Employment Report, AI-adjacent side hustles grew 187% year over year." More concrete than others. Language slightly formal — needed some editing for casual tone.

Time: 2 minutes

Claude — ★★★★☆ Best Structure

Most logical flow. Clear transitions between sections. Best for educational content where clarity matters more than entertainment. Less natural "hook energy" compared to ChatGPT.

Time: 3 minutes

Jasper — ★★★☆☆ Best for Business

Good copywriting structure but felt too salesy for organic content. Best for promotional video scripts rather than organic creator content.

Time: 3 minutes

Script Prompt Template

You are a professional video script writer for YouTube/TikTok. Write a [duration] script for a talking head video.

Topic: [your topic]
Target audience: [describe viewers]

Requirements:
1. Opening 3 seconds must include a hook (question, bold statement, or surprising fact)
2. Body: 3 main points, each with a concrete example or data point
3. Strong closing with a memorable one-liner + call to action
4. Conversational, natural language — like talking to a friend
5. Mark each section with suggested duration (seconds)
6. Suggest B-roll footage cues in brackets

Our Take

  • Best all-rounder → ChatGPT, fastest and most natural output
  • Best for data-driven content → DeepSeek, great statistics and facts
  • Best for educational content → Claude, clearest logical structure
  • Best for promotional → Jasper, built for conversion copy

Whichever tool you use, always add your personal experiences and speaking style. That's what makes AI-written content uniquely yours.
