AI Image Generator Cost Comparison 2026

Jun 15, 2026 路 7 min read

AI image generation pricing varies dramatically across platforms. We broke down the real cost per image for every major AI image generator to help you choose the best value for your content creation needs.

Midjourney 鈥?Best Quality, Mid-Range Price

Midjourney sets the standard for AI image quality. At $10/month for 200 images (~$0.05/image) or $30/month for 900 images (~$0.03/image), it offers excellent value for quality-focused creators.

DALL-E 3 and Stable Diffusion

DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) effectively costs nothing extra if you already use ChatGPT. Stable Diffusion is completely free and open-source 鈥?run it locally with ComfyUI or Automatic1111. Only costs: electricity and your GPU hardware.

Leonardo AI and Adobe Firefly

Leonardo AI offers 150 free daily credits (~30-50 images). Paid plans start at $10/month. Adobe Firefly is included with Creative Cloud and offers 25 free monthly credits with commercial safety guarantees. Best for: businesses concerned about copyright.

Cost Comparison Summary

For $0: Stable Diffusion (local) is unbeatable. For $10-20/month: Leonardo AI or Midjourney Basic offer the best value. For professionals generating 1000+ images monthly, Midjourney Standard is most cost-effective.

Free AI Image Generators Worth Trying

Beyond the paid options, several free AI image generators deserve attention. Bing Image Creator (powered by DALL-E) is completely free with a Microsoft account. Craiyon offers unlimited free generations with ads. Playground AI provides 1,000 free monthly credits. These are excellent starting points for beginners who want to experiment before committing to a paid subscription.
