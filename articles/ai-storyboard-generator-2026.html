Best AI Storyboard Generators for Video Planning 2026

A great video starts with a great plan. Storyboarding — visualizing each shot before filming — is essential for YouTube creators, filmmakers, and animators. AI-powered storyboard generators can now take your script and automatically generate shot-by-shot visual boards in seconds. Here are the best tools for video planning in 2026.

Boords (AI Storyboard Generator)

Boords is the industry leader for AI storyboarding. Paste your script, and it generates a complete storyboard with camera angles, character positions, and scene descriptions. You can customize each frame with simple text prompts. It supports collaboration, comments, and animatic previews. Pricing starts at $29/month for the Pro plan.

Storyboarder (Free + AI Plugin)

Storyboarder by Wonder Unit is a free, open-source storyboarding tool. With the AI plugin, you can generate frames from text descriptions directly within the app. It offers a fast, sketch-style workflow that professional animators love. Export options include PDF, GIF animatics, and Premiere Pro compatible files.

Plot

Plot is designed specifically for video creators on YouTube and TikTok. It combines scriptwriting with AI storyboard generation, allowing you to plan your entire video in one workspace. The AI auto-generates B-roll suggestions and transitions. The free tier includes 3 projects; Pro is $15/month.

ShotPro

ShotPro uses AI to generate photorealistic storyboard frames. It is ideal for product videos and commercial work where visual accuracy matters. You can specify camera lenses, lighting conditions, and even wardrobe. It is on the pricier side at $49/month but includes commercial usage rights.

Camera Blocking AI (Mobile)

This iOS/Android app uses your phone camera to create real-world storyboard frames with AI-generated characters and props. Point your phone at a location, describe the action, and the AI renders a storyboard frame in that setting. It is perfect for location scouting. Free with in-app purchases.

Tips for Better Storyboarding

Start with a solid script — AI storyboard generators are only as good as the input they receive. Break your script into individual shots and describe the action, camera angle, and mood for each. Most tools support shot list export, which you can use as a production checklist. Remember: storyboarding saves time in editing by reducing the need for reshoots and coverage.
