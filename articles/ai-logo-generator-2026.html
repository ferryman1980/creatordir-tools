Free AI Logo Generators 2026: Create Brand Logos Instantly

Jun 13, 2026 · 5 min read

A great logo is the foundation of your brand identity. You don't need to spend hundreds on a designer — these free AI logo generators create professional results in minutes.

1. Looka — Best Overall Free Option

Looka (formerly Logojoy) asks you a few questions about your brand style, colors, and industry, then generates hundreds of logo options. The AI learns your preferences as you select favorites, refining results. Downloading a low-res preview is free. Full resolution and source files require a paid plan, but the free preview is enough to test concepts.

Try Looka →

2. Canva Logo Maker — Free with Limits

Canva's AI logo maker lets you start with a prompt or browse templates. The AI generates logo variations based on your brand name and tagline. Canva's free tier includes thousands of elements and fonts. The downside is that watermarked downloads need a Pro subscription, but screenshots work fine for early-stage testing.

Check Canva Logo Maker →

3. Hatchful by Shopify — Completely Free

Hatchful is 100% free with no hidden charges. Shopify built it for entrepreneurs launching brands. Answer a few questions about your industry, visual style, and logo types, and Hatchful generates dozens of options. You can customize colors, fonts, and layouts before downloading high-resolution PNG files at no cost.

4. Brandmark — AI-Powered Logo Design

Brandmark generates unique logo designs using AI. Enter your business name and industry, and it creates a logo with custom icon, font pairing, and color scheme. The free preview gives you a low-resolution version. It's particularly good for tech and creative brands looking for modern, minimal designs.

Try Brandmark →

How to Choose

For completely free downloads, Hatchful is the clear winner. For the best designs, Looka's AI generates the most professional options. And if you're already using Canva for other design work, their logo maker is the most convenient choice.
