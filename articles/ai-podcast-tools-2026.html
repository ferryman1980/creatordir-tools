Best AI Podcast Tools 2026

Published: 2026-06-22

Podcasting involves many steps: recording, editing, transcription, show notes, and distribution. AI tools streamline every stage.

Top AI Podcast Tools

1. Descript

Descript lets you edit audio by editing text. AI removes filler words, stutters, and silences automatically.

2. Riverside.fm

Riverside records high-quality local audio and video. Its AI produces show clips, transcripts, and summaries.

3. Buzzsprout

Buzzsprout handles hosting and distribution. Its AI generates chapter markers, transcripts, and show notes.

4. Otter.ai

Otter provides real-time transcription and AI meeting notes. Great for interview-based podcasts.

5. Podcastle

Podcastle is an all-in-one podcast platform with AI recording, editing, and magic dust audio enhancement.
