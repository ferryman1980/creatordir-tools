Stable Diffusion Guide: Run AI Image Generation Locally

Jun 11, 2026 · 6 min read · New

Stable Diffusion is the most popular open-source AI image generation model. Unlike Midjourney or DALL-E, it runs on your own computer. No subscriptions, no usage limits, no censorship. Here is how to get started.

Why Choose Stable Diffusion?

  • Completely free — generate unlimited images once set up
  • Privacy — everything runs locally, nothing leaves your computer
  • Control — fine-tune every aspect with ControlNet, LoRA, and custom models
  • Community — 1000s of specialized models on Civitai for every style

Getting Started

Option 1: Automatic1111 WebUI (Recommended)

The most popular interface. One-click installer available for Windows. Supports all features: img2img, inpainting, ControlNet, LoRA, upscaling. Requires a GPU with at least 6GB VRAM for SDXL models.

Option 2: ComfyUI

Node-based interface for advanced users. More complex but more powerful. Better for complex workflows and batch processing.

Option 3: Online Services

If you do not have a powerful GPU, use Clipdrop or Leonardo.ai for free SD generation without local installation.

Best Models to Try First

  • SDXL 1.0 — best all-purpose model, 1024x1024 resolution
  • Realistic Vision — best for photorealistic images
  • DreamShaper — best for artistic and creative styles
