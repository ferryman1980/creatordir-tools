AI Image Generator Comparison 2026: Midjourney vs DALL-E vs Stable Diffusion vs Leonardo

Published: 2026-06-22

Choosing the right AI image generator depends on your needs: artistic quality, speed, control, or budget. Here's our detailed comparison.

Midjourney

Best for: Artistic quality and creative projects.
Price: $10-60/month
Pros: Best image quality, unique style, active community.
Cons: Requires Discord, limited control over composition.

DALL-E 3 (OpenAI)

Best for: Precise prompt following and commercial use.
Price: Included with ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo)
Pros: Excellent at following complex prompts, integrated with ChatGPT.
Cons: Less artistic style variety.

Stable Diffusion

Best for: Customization and control.
Price: Free (open-source)
Pros: Fully customizable, runs locally, countless models.
Cons: Requires technical setup for best results.

Leonardo AI

Best for: Game assets and consistent characters.
Price: Free tier + $10/month
Pros: Fine-tuned models, character consistency, generous free tier.
Cons: Smaller community.

Verdict

Midjourney wins for artistic quality. DALL-E 3 wins for prompt accuracy. Stable Diffusion wins for customization. Leonardo wins for free usage.
