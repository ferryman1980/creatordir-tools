AI Resume Builder 2026: Best CV & Resume Tools

Jun 13, 2026 · 5 min read

Your resume is your first impression on recruiters. AI resume builders help you create professional, ATS-friendly resumes that get you more interviews.

1. Kickresume — Best Overall

Kickresume combines AI writing with professional design templates. The AI Resume Writer generates bullet points based on your job history — just describe your role and it writes achievement-oriented statements. The ATS score checker tells you how well your resume will pass automated screening systems. Templates are modern and recruiter-approved.

Try Kickresume →

2. Rezi — ATS-Optimized Focus

Rezi is built specifically for ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization. Every resume generated follows ATS best practices: standard section headings, keyword optimization, and clean formatting. Rezi analyzes job descriptions and suggests keywords to include. It's the best choice if you're applying to companies that use automated screening.

Check Rezi →

3. Enhancv — Design-First Resumes

Enhancv focuses on visual resume design. Its AI suggests content improvements while maintaining a clean, modern layout. The platform includes personal branding elements like "about me" sections and achievement highlights. It's ideal for creative roles where design matters.

4. Teal — AI + Job Tracking

Teal combines an AI resume builder with a job application tracker. The AI tailors your resume for specific job descriptions, highlighting relevant experience. The Chrome extension works with LinkedIn and job boards to extract requirements and optimize your resume accordingly.

Try Teal →

Quick Tips

Quantify achievements with numbers, tailor each resume to the job description, and keep it to one page unless you have 10+ years of experience. AI tools handle formatting — your job is to provide specific, measurable accomplishments.
