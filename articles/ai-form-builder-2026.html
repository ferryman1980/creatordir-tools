Best AI Form & Survey Builders 2026

Forms are the backbone of audience engagement — email signups, customer feedback, market research, and booking requests. AI-powered form builders now generate intelligent forms that adapt questions based on previous answers, predict drop-off points, and optimize conversion rates automatically. Here are the best tools for creators in 2026.

Typeform (AI Builder)

Typeform remains the gold standard for beautiful, conversational forms. Its AI Builder, introduced in 2025, lets you describe your form in plain English, and it generates a complete multi-step form with conditional logic, branding, and analytics. The AI also suggests question improvements to reduce abandonment. Pricing starts at $29/month.

Jotform AI Builder

Jotform's AI Builder is the most feature-rich option, generating forms from a simple prompt and importing data from existing documents like PDFs and spreadsheets. It supports 10,000+ integrations and offers free plan with 5 forms. The AI can also analyze form responses and generate summary reports automatically.

Tally (Freemium)

Tally is the minimalist's choice — unlimited forms, unlimited responses, and no branding on the free plan. Its AI assistant helps write effective questions and suggests logical branching. Tally is entirely web-based and collaborates in real time. The Pro plan is $29/month and adds file uploads, hidden fields, and advanced logic.

Paperform

Paperform combines AI form generation with a powerful document editor. You can create forms that look like branded landing pages, complete with rich text, images, and videos. The AI suggests form layouts and question phrasing based on your industry. Plans start at $24/month.

Fillout AI

Fillout is a newer player that rivals Typeform in design quality while being more affordable. Its AI builder can clone existing forms from a URL and generate variants for A/B testing. The free tier includes unlimited responses, making it a great starting point. Paid plans begin at $15/month.

How to Choose a Form Builder

Consider your primary use case. For customer satisfaction surveys: Typeform's conversational UI drives higher completion rates. For complex data collection: Jotform's integrations are unmatched. For simple, no-fuss forms: Tally is the best free option. Always set up automated thank-you emails and response notifications regardless of which tool you choose.
