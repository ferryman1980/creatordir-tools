AI Website Builders for Creators 2026: Launch in Hours

Jun 13, 2026 · 5 min read

Creating a website used to mean learning HTML, hiring a developer, or wrestling with page builders. AI website builders have changed that. Now you can describe your site and have it built in minutes.

1. Wix ADI — AI That Builds the Whole Site

Wix's Artificial Design Intelligence (ADI) asks about your business, preferences, and goals, then builds a complete website. It generates pages, writes placeholder content, selects images, and sets up navigation. You can customize everything after, but the AI does the heavy lifting. Wix ADI is particularly good for portfolios, service businesses, and online stores.

Try Wix ADI →

2. 10Web — AI for WordPress

10Web uses AI to build WordPress sites. Enter your business description, and it generates a complete WordPress site with Elementor pages, custom theme, and relevant plugins. The AI also handles hosting and security. It's the fastest way to launch a professional WordPress site if you don't want to deal with setup.

Check 10Web →

3. Durable — One-Click Business Site

Durable generates a complete business website in 30 seconds. Enter your business name and location, and the AI creates a site with generated content, images, and contact forms. It's designed for local businesses and freelancers who need a simple, professional web presence fast.

4. Framer AI — Designer-Friendly

Framer's AI site builder lets you describe your brand and generates a designed site with animations and interactions. It's perfect for creators who want something visually impressive without coding. The AI handles responsive design automatically, and you can publish directly to Framer's hosting.

Try Framer AI →

Pick the Right Builder

Wix ADI is best for feature-rich sites. 10Web wins if you want WordPress. Durable is perfect for a simple 5-minute launch. Framer AI is the choice for design-first creators.
