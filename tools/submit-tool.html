Common questions about listing your AI tool

How long does the review process take?

We review all submissions within 24-48 hours. You will receive an email confirmation once your tool goes live.

Is it free to submit my tool?

Yes! Submitting your AI tool to our directory is completely free. There are no hidden fees or paid placement options.

Can I update my tool listing after submission?

Absolutely. Once your tool is live, you can request updates to the description, URL, pricing, or other details by emailing us.

What types of tools do you accept?

We accept AI-powered tools relevant to content creators, including writing, design, video, audio, marketing, productivity, and development tools.

Can I submit multiple tools?

Yes, you can submit as many tools as you like. Please submit a separate form for each tool.

Why was my tool rejected?

Common reasons include: the tool is not AI-related, is not relevant to creators, has a broken website, or does not meet our quality standards. We will email you with specific feedback.
